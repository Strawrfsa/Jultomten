About Jultomten

Jultomten is the Swedish name for Santa Claus, a gift-giving figure who is a central part of Christmas celebrations in Sweden.

Originally a gnome-like figure from Swedish folklore, the modern Jultomten is a hybrid of the traditional gnome, St. Nicholas, and the Christmas goat (Julbocken), often depicted as a jolly man in red with a white beard who delivers presents on Christmas Eve.
